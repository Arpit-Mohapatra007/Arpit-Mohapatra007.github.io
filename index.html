The Problem

India has over 360 million informal workers, many of whom live in rural and semi-urban areas.

  • No reliable way to find jobs near home (most jobs are urban-centric).
  • No access to legal agreements, leading to wage theft and exploitation.
  • No resumes or skill documentation, limiting long-term growth.
  • Little to no awareness of government skill camps or internships.

The Solution

GrameenWork is a hyperlocal, mobile-first platform that empowers rural job seekers

Key Features

Job Discovery

within 3–15 km radius using smart filters like "walkable only" or "with transport."

Voice & SMS Access

for low-literacy or offline users.

Auto-Biodata Generator

that updates with every job and internship.

Digital Work Agreements

signed via OTP or voice for legal protection.

Skilling + Internship Discovery

via PMKVY, DDU-GKY, and NGOs.

Smart Dashboard

to track experience, feedback, and skill badges.
